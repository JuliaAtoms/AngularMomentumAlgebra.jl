    Energy expressions

    Energy expressions

    Base.Matrix — Type.
    Matrix(op::QuantumOperator,
       spin_cfgs::Vector{<:Configuration{<:SpinOrbital}}[, overlaps])

    Generate the energy-expression associated with the quantum operator op, in the basis of the spin-configurations spin_cfgs, with an optional set of orbital overlaps, specifying any desired non-orthogonalities. The energy expression is generated in a basis-agnostic way by EnergyExpressions.jl and then multipole-expanded.

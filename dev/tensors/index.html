Dipole operator

The dipole operator is a rank-1 Cartesian tensor that may be expressed using the rank-1 spherical tensor:

\[\hat{\vec{r}} \equiv \begin{bmatrix}\hat{x}\\\hat{y}\\\hat{z}\end{bmatrix} \equiv \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}[-\tensor{C}^{(1)}_1 + \tensor{C}^{(1)}_1]\\ \frac{\im}{\sqrt{2}}[\tensor{C}^{(1)}_1 + \tensor{C}^{(1)}_1]\\ \tensor{C}^{(1)}_0 \end{bmatrix}\]
